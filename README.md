# Shiranh Arcade

Professional multi-game browser arcade developed by shiranh.

## Games

- Star Catcher Pro - catch stars, avoid rocks, and protect your lives.
- Neon Gem Rush - collect glowing gems while dodging red sparks.
- Pulse Target Pro - tap glowing targets before the pulse disappears.
- Shiranh Sudoku Pro - solve a big mobile-friendly 9x9 number puzzle with five difficulty levels from Easy to Master.

## Controls

Star Catcher Pro and Neon Gem Rush work with arrow keys, A/D, or touch dragging. Pulse Target Pro works by clicking or tapping targets. Shiranh Sudoku Pro works by tapping a square, then tapping the on-screen number pad.
